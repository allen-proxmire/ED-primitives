# philosophical_background.md
### *The conceptual lineage that motivates ED*

ED is not derived from physics.  
It is motivated by a long lineage of philosophical ideas.

---

## 1. Structuralism
Structure is primary.  
Entities are defined by relations.

ED inherits:

- relation‑first ontology  
- structure‑first explanation  
- identity as derivative  

---

## 2. Minimalism
Ontologies should be:

- lean  
- disciplined  
- non‑redundant  

ED’s 13 primitives reflect this.

---

## 3. Compositionality
Complexity arises from:

- simple ingredients  
- simple rules  
- repeated composition  

ED motifs embody this.

---

## 4. Relational metaphysics
Nothing exists independently.  
Everything exists through participation.

ED formalizes this structurally.

---

## 5. Anti‑substantivalism
Substance is not fundamental.  
Structure is.

ED treats:

- objects  
- fields  
- particles  

as emergent.

---

## 6. Summary
ED is grounded in:

- structuralism  
- minimalism  
- compositionality  
- relational ontology  
- anti‑substantivalism  

These form the **philosophical backbone** of the ontology.

---
