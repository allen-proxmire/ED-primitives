# why_multiplicity.md
### *Why ED requires multiple interacting tendencies*

Multiplicity is essential to ED.  
Without it, the ontology collapses into uniformity.

---

## 1. Multiplicity creates contrast
Contrast requires:

- at least two tendencies  
- at least two directions  
- at least two commitments  

Without multiplicity, no sign‑flip can occur.

---

## 2. Multiplicity prevents collapse
A single tendency produces:

- no structure  
- no boundaries  
- no motifs  
- no horizons  

Multiplicity ensures **rich architecture**.

---

## 3. Multiplicity supports composition
Motifs require:

- multiple gradients  
- multiple channels  
- multiple commitments  

Multiplicity is the **raw material** of structure.

---

## 4. Summary
Multiplicity is essential because:

- contrast requires it  
- structure requires it  
- motifs require it  
- architecture requires it  

Multiplicity is the **source of richness** in ED.

---
