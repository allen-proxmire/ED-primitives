# why_relational_timing.md
### *Why ED treats time as coordination, not background*

ED does not assume time.  
It assumes **relational timing** — encoded by ED‑13.

---

## 1. Time presupposes a background
A universal time requires:

- simultaneity  
- ordering  
- metric structure  

ED assumes none of these.

---

## 2. Timing is coordination
In ED:

> **Timing is the coordination of event chains.**

Events do not occur *in* time.  
Time is a **relation between events**.

---

## 3. Timing emerges from structure
Horizons encode:

- direction  
- influence  
- propagation  

These produce **temporal order**, not temporal background.

---

## 4. Summary
ED treats time as:

- relational  
- structural  
- emergent  
- coordination‑based  

Time is **not** a primitive — coordination is.
